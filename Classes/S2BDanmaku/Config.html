  • Mode

    Mode/Type of the danmaku.

    • normal: 滚动弹幕 (rolling) on bilibili.
    • bottom: 底部弹幕 on bilibili.
    • top: 顶部弹幕 on bilibili.
    • reversed: 逆向弹幕 on bilibili.
    • special: 特殊弹幕 on bilibili.
    • code: 代码弹幕 on bilibili.
    • bas: bilibili animation script.
